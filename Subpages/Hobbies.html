Hobbies & Interests

Hi! I'm Ashley, and in addition to being passionate about software development, I love to spend my time doing various activities that keep me both mentally and physically engaged. Here’s a peek into my hobbies and some of the things that make me, well... me!

Baking and Cooking

Nothing beats the satisfaction of baking my favorite cookies or experimenting with new recipes. I often spend weekends testing out new dishes!

Traveling to Disney World

I'm a huge Disney fan, and every year I try to visit Disney World to recharge and immerse myself in the magic. It’s my perfect escape!

Softball

I love staying active and playing softball. I am on the W&J softball team and I play the outfield. Whether it’s hitting or diving for a catch, it’s always fun to challenge myself and be part of a team!

Kappa Alpha Theta Sorority

Being part of my sorority, Kappa Alpha Theta (KAO), has taught me the importance of sisterhood, community, and leadership. I participate in events and social gatherings.
