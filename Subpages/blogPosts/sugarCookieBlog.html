My Go-To Sugar Cookie Recipe

If you’ve ever wanted a sugar cookie recipe that’s simple, reliable, and always a hit—look no further. I’ve tried a lot of sugar cookie recipes over the years, and this one? This is the one. This recipe has never failed me and I have always recieved compliments on it.

Link to original recipe!

Why I Love This Recipe

  • No chilling required (I am very impatient and it speeds up the time from mix to cookie in my mouth)
  • It only requires basic ingredients you likely already have
  • Crispy edges, chewy centers!

Ingredients You’ll Need

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

How to Make Them

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, and baking powder.
  3. In a larger bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients.
  6. Roll dough into walnut-sized balls and place 2 inches apart on an ungreased baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 8–10 minutes, or until the edges are just golden.
  8. Cool briefly on the baking sheet, then move to a wire rack to cool completely.

Optional Upgrades & Pro Tips

  • Make Round: Roll dough into balls before baking.
  • Add sparkle and YUM: Roll dough in sugar before baking.(Colored sugar is a bonus!)
  • Soft cookies: Take them out at 8 minutes. They’ll firm up as they cool.
  • Freeze-friendly: Shape and freeze dough balls for future baking.(bake them later for fresh cookies whenever!)
  • Add extra YUM: Add 1-2 tablespoons of sugar to the recipe to make them extra sweet!(Warning: can make them less puffy)

Final Thoughts

These cookies are always a win in my book. Whether its Christmas, Easter, football season, a cookout, or the 4th of july, these cookies always hit the spot, which is why they're my go-to. I've recieved countless compliments on them and I needed to share the recipe!

No matter your baking level, this recipe is approachable, fun, and always tasty. Bake a batch and let me know how yours turn out!
