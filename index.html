About Me

I’m a junior at Washington & Jefferson College, majoring in Computing & Information Studies and set to graduate in May 2026. I’m passionate about coding and problem-solving. I have experience in programming in java, building databases, creating simulations in Python, debugging with fellow students as a PAL tutor, and much more!

As a college athlete and lifelong softball player, I’ve developed strong leadership, time management, and teamwork skills that carry into my academic and project work. I take pride in balancing a demanding schedule while maintaining a 3.86 GPA and contributing to both the classroom and my community.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me baking, on the softball field, playing with my dog, or rocking out to some good old 80s music. I’m also a proud member of Kappa Alpha Theta, where I get to connect with amazing people and be part of something bigger. In all, I’m always looking for opportunities to grow, collaborate, and bring energy and dedication to new challenges in tech.
